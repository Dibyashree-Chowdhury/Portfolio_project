Hi, I'm Dibyashree Chowdhury, a Business Analyst with 2.5+ years of experience delivering business solutions across Healthcare IT, Fintech/BFSI, Ed-Tech, and Automotive Technology. I started my professional journey at Cognizant as Process Executive in Clinical Data Management Project, where I developed a strong foundation in SQL data validation, process analysis, and quality assurance. Driven by my interest in solving business problems and delivering technology solutions, I made a planned transition into Business Analysis by completing a Post Graduate Certification in Business Analytics from the E&ICT Academy, MNIT Jaipur.

I am now seeking a long-term opportunity where I can contribute as a Business Analyst while continuing to build data-driven and customer-focused digital solutions.
